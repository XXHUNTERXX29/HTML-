Backstory:

The McLaren 765LT is an extreme, track-focused version of the McLaren 720S, with more power, less weight, and sharper aerodynamics. Launched in 2020 as part of McLaren's "Longtail" series, the 765LT stands for its extended bodywork and enhanced performance. With a 755 HP twin-turbocharged V8 engine and a top speed of over 200 mph, the 765LT is one of the fastest road-legal cars McLaren has ever built. It is revered for its precise handling and blistering acceleration, making it a favorite among driving enthusiasts.
